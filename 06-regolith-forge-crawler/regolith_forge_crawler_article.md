# The Regolith Forge Crawler 'Terramother'

*Category: Off-World Civilisation Tools · Off-World Civilisation Toolkit*

Cities should be waiting for their citizens. The Terramother is a 26-tonne autonomous crawler that eats planetary soil at one end and extrudes civilisation at the other: an intake maw grinds regolith, an electrostatic stack sorts it into minerals, a microwave-plasma kiln sinters the silicates at 1,600 °C with no imported binder, and a nine-metre gantry arm 3D-prints the result into habitat domes, roads, landing pads, and metre-thick radiation walls. One dome every three weeks, powered by a Kilopower-class fission module, with no human within a hundred million kilometres.

In-situ resource utilisation is proven — MOXIE made oxygen from Martian air — and sintered-regolith construction is an active research field with printed test structures on Earth. The autonomy required is the same class now driving terrestrial mining equipment.

**Status: a coordinated program could field a first unit on the Moon within two decades.** Blueprint sheet in this folder.
