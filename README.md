# Off-World Civilisation Toolkit

**Twenty machines that do not exist — yet.** A catalogue of concept studies for spacecraft, off-world infrastructure, black-hole-era instruments, defense systems, and AI robots. Every concept is constrained to known physics: nothing here breaks a law of nature, only the current limits of engineering, materials, and budgets.

Each concept folder contains a short article; flagship concepts additionally include a full research-paper-style concept study (PDF, Harvard referencing), an engineering blueprint sheet (SVG), and detailed image-generation prompts.

## The Catalogue

### Spacecraft & Space Journeys
1. **[The Lagrange Loom](01-lagrange-loom/)** — Every rocket throws away its road.
2. **[The Magsail Cruiser](02-magsail-cruiser/)** — A ship with no engine and no fuel tank.
3. **[The Beam-Rider Interstellar Probe](03-beam-rider-probe/)** — The first starship will weigh less than a coin.
4. **[The Hibernation Transit Pod](04-hibernation-transit-pod/)** — The cheapest cargo to send to Mars is a sleeping human.
5. **[The Orbital Propellant Kelp Farm](05-orbital-propellant-depot/)** — Orbit's first industry will be selling water.

### Off-World Civilisation Tools
6. **[The Regolith Forge Crawler 'Terramother'](06-regolith-forge-crawler/)** — Cities should be waiting for their citizens.
7. **[The Cryo-Volatile Harvester](07-cryo-volatile-harvester/)** — The Moon's poles hide craters where the Sun has never risen — and in that permanent -230 °C darkness, billions of tonnes of ancient ice.
8. **[The Atmospheric Terraforming Seeder](08-terraforming-seeder/)** — Terraforming won't start with mirrors the size of continents.
9. **[The Planetary Power Loop](09-planetary-power-loop/)** — A colony's most fragile organ is its power supply — dust storms starve solar panels, and night always comes.

### Black Holes & Extreme Physics
10. **[The Ergosphere Siphon](10-ergosphere-siphon/)** — A spinning black hole is a flywheel with the mass of a star, and general relativity says the rotational energy is genuinely extractable.
11. **[The Gravitational Lens Telescope Fleet](11-gravitational-lens-fleet/)** — The largest telescope we will ever own is the Sun.
12. **[The Accretion-Disk Weather Buoy](12-accretion-disk-buoy/)** — The most violent weather in the universe surrounds black holes: accretion disks where plasma heats to millions of degrees, magnetic fields snap and reconnect with the energy of stellar flares, and matter spirals through physics no laboratory can recreate.

### Defense Systems
13. **[The Kinetic Chaff Halo](13-kinetic-chaff-halo/)** — Armour that thinks.
14. **[The Mini-Magnetosphere Deflector](14-mini-magnetosphere-deflector/)** — Earth's habitability rests on an invisible organ: the magnetosphere that deflects solar storms.
15. **[The Asteroid Herder](15-asteroid-herder/)** — Planetary defense is the one weapon system every nation wants its rivals to have.
16. **[The Directed-Energy Debris Broom](16-debris-broom/)** — Orbit is filling with shrapnel — millions of fragments too small to track and catch, each moving ten times faster than a rifle bullet.

### Weaponry (Conceptual)
17. **[The Hypervelocity Rail Interceptor](17-rail-interceptor/)** — In orbit, speed is the explosive.
18. **[The Dazzler Constellation](18-dazzler-constellation/)** — The most dangerous moment in any orbital standoff is the one where a nation must choose between doing nothing and destroying something.

### AI Robots
19. **[The Myrmidon Swarm](19-myrmidon-swarm/)** — A ship's hull should heal like skin.
20. **[The Deep-Down Cryobot](20-deep-down-cryobot/)** — Somewhere under Europa's ice, there is an ocean holding twice the water of all Earth's seas, warmed by tidal flexing, in contact with a rocky floor — every ingredient of life's recipe, sealed under kilometres of ice for four billion years.
